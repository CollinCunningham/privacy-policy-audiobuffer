1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.
The AudioBuffer app is designed to respect your privacy. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software. No personal information, such as your name, email address, or location, is ever requested or collected.

2. Audio Recordings

All audio recordings made through the app are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to your recordings, and they are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. You have full control over your files, including the ability to delete them at any time.

3. Device Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the app requires access to your device's Microphone. This permission is used solely to record audio at your request. We do not record audio without your explicit action.
